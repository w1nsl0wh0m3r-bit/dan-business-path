## 3. Unit Economics
**Small commercial grounds company target:**
- Revenue: **$1.5M-$5M**.
- Gross margin: **30%-45%**; labor and fuel are the swing factors.
- EBITDA/SDE: **8%-18%**. Commercial maintenance often lower margin but higher recurring quality.
- SDE target: **$200K-$500K**.
- Entry multiple: **2.0x-3.5x SDE**; 4x only for clean management, recurring contracts, and low customer concentration.
- Payback: **3-5 years**.

**Contract math estimate:** HOA/community maintenance contracts can range from **$20K-$120K+ annually** depending on acreage, mowing frequency, plant beds, irrigation, and snow. A 100-200 unit HOA can anchor a crew route but may be bid-competitive.

## 7. Acquisition Targets
Look for:
- 70%+ recurring contracted revenue.
- Contract renewal history and gross margin by account.
- Snow removal capability in New England to offset seasonality.
- Crew leaders who can run without owner daily dispatch.
- Modern routing/time tracking or willingness to implement Aspire/LMN/ServiceTitan-like ops stack.

Red flags:
- Underbid municipal/HOA contracts with no inflation escalators.
- High H-2B exposure without process.
- Equipment fleet underinvested or hidden capex.
- Seller owns client relationships personally.
