## Working Capital Edge in Frozen Dessert

### Inventory: Simpler Than Candy

Ice cream/gelato inventory is simpler than Swedish candy:
- Mix base + flavorings (perishable, 2-4 week shelf life fresh)
- Less SKU complexity than candy (30-50 flavors vs 200+ SKUs)
- But: fresh-made-daily models (Handel's, Botolino) require daily production discipline

### The Real WC Edge: Seasonality

For NE targets, seasonality is the negotiating lever:
- Seller presents TTM EBITDA inflated by May-Sep peak
- Nov-Mar revenue can be 20-30% of peak months
- **Always demand monthly P&L** and re-underwrite on off-peak months
- The true run-rate EBITDA is often 70-80% of what the seller presents

For FL/TX targets, this edge doesn't exist — year-round demand.

### Gift Card Float

Same as candy/restaurants — unredeemed balances transfer as liability.
