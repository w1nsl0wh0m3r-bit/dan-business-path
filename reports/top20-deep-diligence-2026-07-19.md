### 1. Leather Spa ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Category:** Luxury Repair & Circular Care | **Conviction: 10** | **Status: TARGET**

**Ownership:** Family-owned. Founder Carlos Mesquita, now operated by David Mesquita (son). 30+ years operating history. Originally "Shoe Service Plus." Zero PE/VC signal.

**Operations:**
- Multiple NYC locations (Midtown, Downtown, potentially Westchester)
- Services: luxury handbag, shoe, leather repair + restoration
- Neiman Marcus repair/restoration partner (confirmed via NM ESG report Oct 2022)
- High-end positioning — Louboutin, Chanel, Hermès clientele

**Why Acquire:**
- Generational succession risk — David Mesquita is second generation; will the third want it?
- Premium brand equity built over 30 years
- Neiman Marcus partnership = enterprise contract, not just retail
- Circular economy tailwind — luxury repair is booming as sustainability narrative grows
- Service is non-substitutable: you can't get this quality elsewhere easily

**Acquisition Path:** Direct relationship approach to David Mesquita. No broker market for this. The pitch: modernize + expand to other affluent markets (Boston, Miami, LA) while preserving brand.

**Economics (proxy):**
- Revenue: est. $2-4M/year (multiple NYC locations, premium pricing, Neiman Marcus contract)
- Margin: 35-40% (service business, low COGS, skilled labor)
- SDE: $500K-$1.2M
- Acquisition price: $1.5-3M (3-4x SDE)
- Capex: minimal (existing infrastructure)

**Next Action:** Direct approach to David Mesquita. Frame as "growth partnership" not buyout. First call: explore appetite for expansion to Boston/Chicago.
