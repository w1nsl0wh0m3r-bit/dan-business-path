Summary:
The top investment-quality names are AI compliance admin, self/portable storage, generator maintenance, distressed franchise portfolios, and men’s health/TRT.
Recurring/contract revenue is the ranking separator: storage, compliance software, generator service plans, and medical/wellness memberships beat discretionary retail/F&B.
Most food concepts should fall despite decent operator availability because exits are capped, recurrence is habit-based not contractual, and unit economics are fragile.
Medspa/GLP-1/TRT have real platform potential, but only if regulatory structure, provider bench, retention, and compliance-safe marketing are proven upfront.
Best near-term diligence: source one generator dealer, one portable-storage operator, one distressed franchise package, and one compliance-route software wedge before touching another retail concept.
