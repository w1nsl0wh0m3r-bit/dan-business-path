Two arenas. Three entry paths. One decision system.

Services or multi-unit first. Category second.

Dan’s edge is real-world business judgment — consumer retail, distribution, manufacturing exposure, and PE underwriting. So the dashboard starts with two arenas, then asks whether each category is best built, franchised, or bought.

The two arenas

Arena 1

Services

Recurring local service businesses: route density, customer books, skilled field labor, compliance/maintenance, home and property systems.

Default posture: buy first, franchise second, DIY only selectively.

Arena 2

Multi-unit

Repeatable consumer-facing units: food, retail, education, wellness, specialty trade retail, membership concepts.

Default posture: DIY first where testable, franchise if system value is real, acquisition/resale selectively.
