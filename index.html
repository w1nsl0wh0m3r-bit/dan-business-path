Current strategic take.

Current priority wedge: property-manager compliance routes + affluent-home systems: Backflow, fire/life-safety, hood cleaning, grease trap, stormwater, and AED compliance are tracked manually by many property managers. The software/calendar layer can become the acquisition channel for the service work.
