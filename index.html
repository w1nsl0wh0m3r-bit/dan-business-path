Current strategic take.

Current priority wedge: property-manager compliance routes + affluent-home systems: Backflow, fire/life-safety, hood cleaning, grease trap, stormwater, and AED compliance are tracked manually by many property managers. The software/calendar layer can become the acquisition channel for the service work.

Services / acquisition

Priority vein: property-manager compliance routes — fire/life-safety, backflow, kitchen hood, grease trap, stormwater — plus drainage/irrigation as affluent-home exterior systems.

Multi-unit / DIY

The universe now covers 100+ food, beverage, beauty, aesthetics, fitness, kids, pet, retail, specialty-counter, apparel, home, and CPG/light-manufacturing concepts.

Franchise

Middle path only when the operating system is worth the drag. Hood cleaning and Metal Supermarkets deserve FDD checks; generic seasonal service franchises need skepticism.
