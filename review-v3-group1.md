## Summary — honest top 10 at Dan’s scale

1. **Fire/life-safety inspections** — highest-quality recurring compliance route; real but modest multiple spread.
2. **Septic / sewer inspection** — best ugly-business cash-on-cash profile with durable demand.
3. **Mobile drug testing / DOT** — exceptional low-capex payback; must be built into employer contracts, not one-off collections.
4. **Locksmith / access control** — cheap entry, commercial stickiness, and smart-access tailwind.
5. **Metal Supermarkets** — strong Dan edge and four-wall economics, but no franchise resale arbitrage.
6. **Water filtration / softening** — attractive if consumables/service recurrence is already proven.
7. **Contractor supply distribution** — Dan edge is real; only works with clean customer-level data and disciplined inventory.
8. **Crawlspace/basement waterproofing** — good margins, but exit should be underwritten to small-business buyers, not Groundworks/KKR fantasy multiples.
9. **Premium/mobile pet grooming** — fast route-book payback, capped by groomer labor and van utilization.
10. **Estate / property management** — strong if systematized; dangerous if seller trust is the whole product.

The corrected ranking pushes **low-capex compliance routes and boring counter/service operations** above the prettier membership/franchise concepts. At $200K–$500K SDE, Dan should optimize for fast payback, clean transferability, and small-buyer exit math — not PE-platform exit multiples.
