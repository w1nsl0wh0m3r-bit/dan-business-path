## Summary

### Concepts that genuinely deserve top-10 status for Dan

1. **Metal Supermarkets** — clear top idea. Specialty retail + B2B repeat demand + franchise systems + inventory/financial underwriting is the cleanest overlap with Dan's PE skillset.
2. **Estate / property management** — strong recurring-revenue acquisition thesis if relationship transfer and client concentration diligence pass.
3. **Specialty coffee / espresso bar** — Dan-sector consumer retail, but only as a disciplined acquisition of an existing throughput machine, not a lifestyle cafe.
4. **Medical equipment / mobility retail / 101 Mobility** — aging-in-place has real structural demand; keep comparing franchise vs independent acquisition.
5. **Swim school** — viable consumer/multi-unit thesis if acquired/resale/hosted-pool; avoid dedicated pool greenfield risk.
6. **Pet grooming salon** — attractive recurring consumer service with local brand and multi-unit potential; labor retention is the entire deal.

### Borderline but not top-10 without special facts

- **Crawlspace/basement waterproofing** — good market, but Dan is buying into home-services sales/labor risk.
- **Senior home safety modifications** — sensible as a service-line within mobility/home-modification, not as a standalone.
- **Premium bagel** — fun and on-strategy from a consumer lens, but too much food/labor/lease risk to rank highly before a cheap pilot.
- **Summer camp** — consumer fit is decent, but child-safety and seasonality make it a proof-first idea.

### Overrated for Dan specifically

The dashboard appears to over-rank several businesses because it rewards recurring compliance/route economics without fully penalizing Dan-fit. **Backflow, fire/life-safety, kitchen hood, document shredding, dryer vent, radon, irrigation, mosquito/tick, and battery/generator service** may be good small-business categories for the right operator, but they mostly put Dan into technician-route, licensing, or compliance-heavy businesses where his consumer/retail/multi-unit edge is diluted. If Dan later relaxes the route-service ban, fire/life-safety and backflow are the only two I would reopen.
