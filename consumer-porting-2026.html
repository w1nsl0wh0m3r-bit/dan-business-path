Financing read.

SBA unlock

SBA primary sources confirm eligible borrowers can coordinate up to $5M of 7(a) plus up to $5M of 504 financing, effective July 4, 2026. That changes the ceiling for multi-unit acquisitions, owner-occupied sites, and equipment-heavy concepts.

Discipline

The new cap does not make weak stores good. Use it for cash-flowing acquisitions, real estate/equipment-backed plans, and sequenced rollouts where DSCR works after paid management.
