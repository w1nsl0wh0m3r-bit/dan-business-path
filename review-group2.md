## Brief summary

### Concepts from ranks 21–40 that genuinely deserve top-10 consideration for Dan

1. **Local specialty distribution** — strongest Dan-specific fit if it is a real counter/advice/inventory moat, not generic low-margin wholesale.
2. **Garage door service** — boring, urgent, dispatchable, SBA-friendly; the best trade-services candidate if CAC and technician quality are clean.
3. **Tennis / padel academy** — top-10 only through acquisition of an existing club with members and real utilization, not a ground-up padel bet.
4. **Gymnastics / ninja academy** — compelling youth-membership platform if safety, coach bench, and facility utilization diligence clear.
5. **Music lessons academy** — fits Dan’s consumer/multi-unit operating edge; must prove students belong to the studio, not a few beloved teachers.
6. **Water filtration / softening** — worth keeping in the mix only when recurrence is real through installed-base service, consumables, and rentals.

### Overrated for Dan specifically

- **Grease trap pumping, pet waste removal, pool service** — recurring routes look good on paper but violate Dan’s stated no-route-service filter.
- **Commercial refrigeration / AIM Act and hurricane shutters** — attractive structural demand, but too technical/regulatory/geography-specific for Dan’s first platform.
- **Special needs enrichment** — important category, wrong risk profile; safeguarding and therapy-adjacent boundaries are existential.
- **Lash/brow, waxing, vet-adjacent wellness** — consumer recurring revenue is seductive, but labor churn, liability, franchise royalty drag, and saturation make them second-tier at best.
- **Landscape lighting** — good bootstrap side business, not a serious acquisition platform for Dan.
