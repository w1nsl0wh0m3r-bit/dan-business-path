Summary:
1. The strongest investment theses are recurring/contract-heavy platforms: septic, estate management, swim school, pet wellness, Metal Supermarkets, and specialty distribution.
2. Exit multiple potential materially changes the ranking: boring B2B/compliance routes beat sexy consumer concepts when they can be bought at ~3x and sold at 6–8x.
3. Operator hiring is not the blocker for most categories; the real blockers are transferability, contract quality, customer concentration, and whether SDE survives paid management.
4. Specialty coffee, premium pet retail, blinds, and project showrooms are lower priority because recurrence and exit multiples are capped.
5. Re-rank Dan’s diligence queue around repeat revenue plus exit arbitrage, not personal operating fit alone.
