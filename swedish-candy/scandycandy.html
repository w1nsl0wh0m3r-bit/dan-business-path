Verdict: WATCH — conditional approach, but not at current trajectory. Confidence: Medium. Strong brand momentum and authentic founders, but the window for a minority-growth-partner deal is narrow and closing. The more likely entry is a later-stage growth check once they hit operational strain (inventory, multi-store management, working capital) — likely 6–12 months out. It has viral traction, genuine Swedish founder authenticity, clean infrastructure (Shopify + M365 + Klaviyo), and is expanding into a 4th market (Sarasota). The Swedish candy trend is still in expansion phase — not yet saturated in SFL. The competitive window in South Florida is open but GoYummy is moving aggressively (Boca, Palm Beach Gardens, Fort Lauderdale, Coconut Grove under construction). Why Dan? Dan's PE discipline, SFL geographic fit, and growth-equity skillset match what this business will need in 6–12 months: working capital for inventory, multi-unit operational systems, and someone who can professionalize the back office without killing the founder energy.
