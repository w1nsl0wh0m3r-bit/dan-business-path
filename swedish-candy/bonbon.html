Founder / Ownership

  • Founded in 2017 by three Swedish friends: Selim Adira, Robert “Bobby” Persson, and Leo / Leonard Schaltz.
  • Selim Adira is described in public snippets/PR as co-founder and Chief Product Officer; Leo / Leonard Schaltz as co-founder and CEO; Robert / Bobby Persson is consistently listed as co-founder.
  • Sara S. Ciliberto appears as COO / Partner, a sign BonBon has added operating depth beyond the founders.

Origin / Background

  • First Lower East Side retail store opened in 2018 after the founders felt U.S. gummies did not match Swedish standards.
  • BBB / trademark artifacts point to BonBonNYC LLC and business start in October 2017.
  • Founder-led with no confirmed PE/VC ownership or sale process.

News / Buzz

  • BonBon appears to have helped create the U.S. Swedish-candy zeitgeist. Its own site claims #BonBon has been searched over 3.1B times.
  • External coverage links the 2024 BUBS/Swedish candy shortage and TikTok haul wave to BonBon-originating content.
  • TODAY/NBC and other coverage describe lines around stores and ecommerce momentum.

Social Accounts / Followers

  • TikTok @bonbonnyc: search snippet showed ~49.7K followers / 393.5K likes.
  • Instagram @bonbonnyc_: search snippet showed ~71.9K followers.
  • Owned audience is solid; earned UGC/category meme ownership is the bigger asset.

Revenue / Scale Signals

  • ZoomInfo/Datanyze-style baseline cited ~$10.3M revenue and ~55 employees; treat as directional only.
  • With 6-7 stores, ecommerce, wholesale, and a viral order spike, the revenue estimate is plausible but unverified.
  • Company/location materials reference wholesale reach into 3,000+ retailers.

Openings / Pipeline

  • 2024: Red Hook hybrid warehouse/candy library opened at 66 Degraw St on weekends.
  • 2025: Sag Harbor location announced/opened.
  • 2026: PR references seven retail locations across New York and Connecticut; Greenwich CT appears open or imminent but not in current map rows.

Dan Read / Diligence Notes

  • Do not chase as a normal SMB acquisition.
  • Only interesting through warm founder access for minority growth capital / strategic partner angle.
  • Use as benchmark for what a polished Swedish candy retail + CPG platform can become.
