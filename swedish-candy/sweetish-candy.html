Founder / Ownership

Origin / Background

  • Opened original Lancaster store in 2019 after Swedish vacation/category inspiration.
  • Moved to larger 301 N Queen location in 2022; added broader treats, micro-cafe, Scandinavian gifts/pantry.

News / Buzz

  • Marketplace reported a viral TikTok drove web order queue from ~30-50/day to thousands, including 10,000 orders within a few days.
  • Snack & Bakery reported #SwedishCandy exceeded 120M TikTok views by mid-2024 and “buy Swedish candy” searches rose 240% YoY.

Openings / Pipeline
