Sockerbit

OG Swedish candy retailer turned CPG/wholesale platform. Current map posture: Watch / retail carveout only. This page separates verified map facts from directional founder/social/revenue signals so the comp can be audited cleanly.

Founder / Ownership

  • Founders: Florence Baras and Stefan Ernberg.
  • Sockerbit says the couple met while working at a candy company in Europe, moved to the U.S., and founded Sockerbit in 2010 around the Swedish lördagsgodis ritual.

Origin / Background

  • NYC flagship opened around 2010/2011 and is now closed.
  • LA West 3rd Street opened around 2014 and remains the core physical store.
  • The business has shifted heavily toward CPG/wholesale and better-for-you packaged candy.

News / Buzz

  • Sockerbit is now a distribution story: Target, World Market, Wegmans, The Paper Store, Amazon, ecommerce, and specialty retailers.
  • FoodNavigator covered the Target deal and better-for-you positioning.

Social Accounts / Followers

  • Instagram @sockerbitnyc: search snippet showed ~42K followers and “OG Swedish Candy Importers Since 2010.”
  • TikTok creator reviews exist, but Target/wholesale is the stronger signal than owned social.

Revenue / Scale Signals

  • No revenue disclosure.
  • Company site says wholesale to 3,000+ locations and 1,600 Target doors in winter 2025.
  • Directional split after Target likely majority CPG/wholesale + DTC, not physical retail.

Openings / Pipeline

  • LA store verified open.
  • Pasadena / One Colorado coming Spring 2026 in current map.
  • NYC West Village location is closed.

Dan Read / Diligence Notes

  • Not a small candy-shop acquisition anymore.
  • Potentially interesting only as retail licensing/JV if founders want to extend experiential stores while focusing on CPG.
