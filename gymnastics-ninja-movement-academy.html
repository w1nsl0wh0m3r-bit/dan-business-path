1. Category Definition & Demand Map

  • This business provides specialized local services around gymnastics / ninja / movement academy: facility/class operations, compliance inspections, software/admin workflows, clinical care, or urgent restoration depending on category.
  • Customers are mixed B2C/B2B; underwriting should favor repeat B2B, subscription, referral, or compliance-driven demand over one-off consumer traffic.
  • Demand drivers: regulation, safety, healthcare adoption, youth enrichment, insurance events, and local service fragmentation.
  • Geographic relevance: NE and NY/CT/NJ are attractive because density supports routes/classes; SFL is relevant where healthcare, restoration, and compliance demand is strong.
  • Market size: use cited third-party market references below; where exact vertical data is unavailable, figures are explicitly labeled estimate.

4. Path-Specific Buybox

What makes a GOOD acquisition target in this category?

  • Revenue range: $900K-$1.8M, with no single customer/referral source above 25%-30%.
  • SDE range: $160K-$360K, verified against tax returns and adjusted for a real manager salary.
  • Years in business: 5+ years with clean reviews and documented SOPs.
  • Route density / customer count: enough local density to keep techs/coaches/providers utilized without owner heroics.
  • Key assets to verify: CRM/customer list, licenses/certifications, equipment, vendor contracts, reviews, staff retention, insurance/referral relationships.
  • Red flags to avoid: owner is primary producer, undocumented revenue, regulatory gray area, weak insurance, high ad dependency, or receivables aging.

What makes a good FRANCHISE candidate?

  • Top franchise/brand comps: The Little Gym, Ninja Nation, My Gym, KidStrong, local gymnastics clubs, youth ninja gyms.
  • FDD highlight: confirm Item 19 financial performance representations; do not rely on marketing AUV screenshots.
  • Unit economics from franchise disclosure: use franchise fee/royalty/investment only as diligence starting points; independent acquisition can be cheaper and less constrained.

5. Competitive Landscape

  • Competitors / roll-ups: The Little Gym, Ninja Nation, My Gym, KidStrong, local gymnastics clubs, youth ninja gyms.
  • PE activity: stronger in restoration and field-service software; fragmented local operators remain common in youth movement, AED/compliance routes, and clinics.
  • Fragmentation level: high locally; national brands win brand search but local owner-operators still dominate many markets.
  • Moat: certifications, reviews, referral relationships, compliance documentation, route density, and speed-to-service.

6. Financing, Valuation & Capital Discipline

  • Typical deal structure: asset purchase with seller note/earnout; holdback for licensing, AR, and transition risk.
  • SBA eligibility and terms: operating businesses with positive tax-return cash flow are usually better SBA candidates than pre-revenue startups.
  • Down payment required at Dan's scale: roughly $50K-$200K for $500K-$2.0M purchase price at 10% equity plus fees/working capital.
  • Payback period at base case: 2-4 years if manager-adjusted SDE is real.
  • Honest multiple arbitrage: buy around 3.5x verified SDE and exit around 4.0x only if systems, depth, and recurrence improve.

7. 90-Day Roadmap (If Dan Pursues This)

  • Phase 1 (Days 1-30): Market mapping in Boston/NY/NJ/CT/SFL, broker search, franchise FDD requests, and 5 operator calls.
  • Phase 2 (Days 31-60): Operator partner search, review 3 P&Ls, build manager-adjusted model, and validate local competitor density.
  • Phase 3 (Days 61-90): Submit one IOI/LOI on acquisition target or kill the category with written reasons.

8. Kill Criteria

  • Cannot verify three years of revenue and SDE from tax returns.
  • Owner is the only meaningful producer/salesperson.
  • Paid manager wipes out more than 50% of SDE.
  • Regulatory/licensing risks cannot be transferred or solved within 90 days.
  • Customer acquisition depends on paid ads with weak repeat behavior.
  • Purchase price exceeds 4.5x verified SDE.
